## Local Knowledge Packet

The local synthesis in `docs/wiki/paper/WIKI-P-024.md` keeps these reusable
behaviors:

- make every slide a single claim plus proof object plus non-claim boundary;
- preserve evidence/source boundaries while improving delivery;
- replace internal jargon with audience-action language unless precision would
  be lost;
- give diagrams, charts, and formulas explicit reading paths;
- use comparisons as diagnostic readouts, not catalogs;
- validate visual style through rendered review, not only mechanical layout
  checks;
- record convergence by issue, fix, verification, and stop/continue decision.
